Hirotaka KAMEKO received the B.Eng., M.Eng., and D.Eng. degrees from the University of Tokyo in 2013, 2015, and 2018, respectively. He is currently an Assistant Professor at the Academic Center for Computing and Media Studies, Kyoto University. His research interests include natural language processing and game AI. He is a member of ANLP and IPSJ. 2018年東京大学大学院工学系研究科博士課程修了． 博士 (工学)． 同年より京都大学学術情報メディアセンター助教． 自然言語処理，ゲームAI等に関する研究に従事． 言語処理学会，情報処理学会各会員．
